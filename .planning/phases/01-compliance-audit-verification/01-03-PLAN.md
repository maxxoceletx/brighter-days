**patient-location-protocol.md** — SOP for CA-only patient location verification:

Per B&P Code 2290.5(a)(1): services can only be provided when the patient is physically located in California. This protocol documents how to verify this at each session.

Structure:
1. Legal basis: B&P 2290.5(a)(1) — patient must be physically in CA at time of service
2. Why this matters: Valentina is licensed in CA only; treating a patient in another state without that state's license is practicing medicine without a license
3. Pre-session verification:
   - At intake: Collect patient's home address (must be CA) and typical session location
   - Before each session: Verbal confirmation script — "Can you confirm you are currently physically located in California?"
4. Documentation:
   - Record patient's confirmed location in session notes (city/state, not full address)
   - If patient states they are NOT in CA: Do not proceed with session. Document. Reschedule.
   - If patient is traveling: Session must be rescheduled to when they return to CA (no exceptions)
5. Edge cases:
   - Patient near state border: Verbal confirmation required
   - Patient traveling within CA: Acceptable, document city
   - Patient calling from work vs. home: Either is fine as long as in CA
6. Tebra documentation: Where to note location in the session record
7. Annual review: Protocol reviewed annually and updated if laws change
